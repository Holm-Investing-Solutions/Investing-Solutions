We collect basic account information when users create an account, including:

Holm Capital currently provides free account access.

If subscription-based services are introduced in the future, we may collect additional billing and subscription-related information through secure third-party payment providers.

No online system can guarantee absolute security.

Users are responsible for protecting their login credentials.

5. Data Retention

We retain account information while your account remains active or as needed for legal or operational purposes.

Users may request account deletion by contacting us.

This Privacy Policy may be updated as platform features evolve.

Continued use of the platform after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
